# Product Technical Diligence

Assess whether the product works, whether the team can keep shipping, and whether the system can scale without hidden operational fragility.

## Review Areas

- product clarity and user value
- roadmap realism and prioritization
- architecture and dependency risk
- engineering process and release quality
- implementation burden and services drag
- security and reliability signals
- key-person and vendor concentration

Read `references/checklist.md` for the detailed pass.

## Working Rules

- Distinguish between `demo quality` and `production quality`.
- Treat roadmap promises without staffing or milestone support as low-confidence.
- If security is asserted but no audits, controls, or incident process appear, mark the posture as unproven.
- If product differentiation depends on proprietary data, confirm where the data comes from and who owns it.
