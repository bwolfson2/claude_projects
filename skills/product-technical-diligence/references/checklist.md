# Product and Technical Checklist

## Product

- Is the product solving a painful, repeated workflow?
- How much implementation work is required per customer?
- Is the roadmap focused or overloaded?

## Engineering

- Are releases frequent and predictable?
- Is there evidence of testing, QA, or incident response?
- Is architecture simple enough for the current stage?

## Security and Reliability

- Are there policies, audits, penetration tests, or control descriptions?
- Is customer data handling described clearly?
- Are uptime, support burden, or incident themes visible?

## Team Risk

- Does critical knowledge sit with one founder or one engineer?
- Are there outsourced dependencies that could block delivery?
- Is there hiring pressure in core technical roles?

## Red Flags

- roadmap detached from team capacity
- high implementation or customization burden
- security assertions without evidence
- architecture that appears overbuilt or under-documented
- core product value still delivered manually
