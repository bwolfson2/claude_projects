# Project Categories

## Hiring

Talent acquisition projects for fund team and portfolio companies.

Examples:
- Fund team roles (analysts, associates, partners, operations)
- Portfolio company executive searches
- Advisor and board member recruitment
- Contractor and consultant sourcing

Lifecycle: open → sourcing → screening → interviewing → offer → closed_hired | closed_unfilled

## Research

Thematic research, market analysis, and deep-dive investigations.

Examples:
- Sector landscape analysis (AI infra, fintech, etc.)
- Competitive benchmarking
- Market sizing and TAM studies
- Technology trend analysis
- Regulatory impact research
- LP/fundraising market research

Lifecycle: scoping → active → synthesis → published | archived

## Conversations

Tracked relationship threads across platforms (email, Slack, WhatsApp, Signal).

Examples:
- Ongoing founder relationships
- LP relationship management
- Advisor check-ins
- Co-investor coordination
- Vendor and service provider threads

Lifecycle: active → dormant → archived

## Operations

Administrative and operational projects that keep the fund running.

Examples:
- Finance and accounting systems
- HR and people operations
- Office management and facilities
- Compliance and regulatory filings
- Communications and brand
- IT and infrastructure

Lifecycle: active → blocked → completed | archived

## Legal/Corp

Legal, corporate governance, and regulatory projects.

Examples:
- Fund formation and governance
- LP agreements and documentation
- Investment agreements and closings
- Corporate policies and procedures
- Regulatory reporting (SEC, state)
- Legal audit and compliance

## Product

Fund systems, tools, and internal software products.

Examples:
- Deal management system
- Portfolio monitoring dashboard
- Data pipeline and analytics
- AI/ML tools for analysis
- Document management
- CRM and pipeline tools

## Fundraising

Capital raising and LP engagement projects.

Examples:
- Fund marketing materials
- LP prospecting and outreach
- Fund performance reporting
- Investor relations updates
- Fundraising strategy and execution
- Limited partner communication campaigns

## Portfolio

Portfolio company support and monitoring initiatives.

Examples:
- Portfolio performance tracking
- Value-add programs (recruiting, sales, engineering)
- Board support and coordination
- Exit strategy planning
- Portfolio analytics and benchmarking
- Founder support and networking initiatives
