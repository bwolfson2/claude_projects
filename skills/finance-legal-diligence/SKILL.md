# Finance Legal Diligence

Focus on whether the company is financially understandable and legally clean enough to underwrite. If the dataroom is thin, say so clearly.

## Review Areas

- revenue quality and concentration
- burn, runway, and financing dependence
- forecast realism and cash needs
- cap table, SAFEs, notes, and option overhang
- contract concentration and non-standard obligations
- IP ownership, employment, and compliance posture

Read `references/checklist.md` for the detailed pass.

## Working Rules

- Distinguish booked revenue from contracted revenue and from pipeline.
- If margins are discussed without direct cost allocation, mark them as approximate.
- Treat cap tables as incomplete until financing instruments and option pools reconcile.
- Treat legal summaries as unverified unless the underlying agreements are present.

Use `assets/finance-legal-findings-template.md` for the output.
