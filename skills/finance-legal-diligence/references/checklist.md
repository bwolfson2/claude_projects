# Finance and Legal Checklist

## Financial Quality

- Are statements current and internally consistent?
- Is recurring revenue clearly separated from services or one-time work?
- Are burn and runway consistent with payroll and cash balance?

## Forecast

- Are assumptions explicit?
- Is hiring tied to cash needs?
- Does the forecast depend on unusually aggressive new bookings?

## Capitalization

- Is the cap table current?
- Are SAFEs, notes, warrants, or side letters included?
- Is there an option pool increase or other hidden dilution risk?

## Legal

- Are major customer contracts and key vendor terms present?
- Is IP clearly assigned to the company?
- Are there privacy, employment, tax, or licensing issues visible?

## Red Flags

- weak reconciliation between statements and KPI files
- runway story that depends on immediate fundraising
- customer agreements with outsized liability or termination rights
- unclear IP assignment from founders or contractors
- missing financing documents for instruments on the cap table
