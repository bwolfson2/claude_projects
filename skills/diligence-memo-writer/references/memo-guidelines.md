# Memo Guidelines

## Recommended Order

1. Recommendation
2. Company snapshot
3. Why this can work
4. Why this may fail
5. Critical diligence findings
6. Open items that could change the decision
7. Suggested next step

## Style

- Keep it short enough for a partner to scan quickly.
- Use direct language.
- Avoid jargon that hides uncertainty.
- Put the most decision-relevant evidence in the first screenful.

## Common Failure Modes

- restating management narrative without challenge
- burying critical red flags deep in the memo
- mixing unsupported assumptions with hard evidence
- writing a long summary instead of a recommendation document
