# Prompt Recipes

## Full Dataroom Review

Use `$startup-diligence-orchestrator` to review the dataroom at `<path>`. Start with inventory, route work across commercial, product/technical, and finance/legal, then produce an evidence-backed recommendation plus a prioritized question list.

## Coverage and Gaps Only

Use `$dataroom-intake` on `<path>` and return only the manifest summary, likely high-value files, and missing documents that block a thorough diligence review.

## Commercial Deep Dive

Use `$commercial-diligence-review` on the dataroom materials at `<path>` and assess traction quality, customer concentration, retention, pipeline credibility, and market-size claims with file-path citations.

## Product and Technical Deep Dive

Use `$product-technical-diligence` on the materials at `<path>` and assess product maturity, architecture risk, security posture, and key-person dependency with evidence from the dataroom.

## Finance and Legal Deep Dive

Use `$finance-legal-diligence` on the materials at `<path>` and assess revenue quality, burn and runway, financing complexity, contract risk, IP ownership, and documentation gaps.

## Memo Synthesis

Use `$diligence-memo-writer` to convert the completed findings into an investment-committee memo with recommendation, critical findings, and decision-changing open questions.
