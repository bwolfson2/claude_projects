# Commercial Diligence Review

Focus on whether the company can win customers efficiently and retain them at attractive economics. Challenge narrative claims with usage, retention, and revenue evidence whenever possible.

## Review Areas

- problem severity and buyer urgency
- ICP clarity and segment focus
- funnel quality and sales repeatability
- customer concentration and expansion evidence
- churn, retention, and cohort durability
- competitive differentiation and pricing power
- realism of TAM and growth claims

Read `references/checklist.md` for the detailed test list.

## Working Rules

- Prefer raw exports, cohort tables, or board reporting over deck claims.
- If pipeline quality is discussed without stage definitions or conversion history, mark it weak evidence.
- If customer logos appear without contract value, renewal, or usage proof, mark them as low-confidence traction signals.
- Tie every conclusion to evidence and state what would falsify it.
