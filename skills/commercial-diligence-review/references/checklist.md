# Commercial Checklist

## Demand

- What specific pain point is being solved?
- Who is the buyer and who is the user?
- Is there evidence of budget ownership and short sales cycles?

## Traction

- What are current customers, ACVs, and deployment status?
- Is revenue recurring, usage-based, or project-heavy?
- Are renewals, expansion, and net retention visible?

## GTM

- What channels are producing pipeline?
- Is pipeline weighted toward founder-led selling?
- Are sales cycles and win rates improving?

## Market

- Is the claimed market large enough for venture outcomes?
- Is the market-size logic bottom-up or just a broad top-down slide?
- Are there adjacent incumbents or fast followers that compress pricing?

## Red Flags

- concentration in one or two customers
- unclear retention metrics
- pipeline dominated by pilots
- TAM slide without buyer segmentation
- growth story unsupported by cohorts or conversion data
